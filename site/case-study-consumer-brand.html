Consumer UX Interaction Design Prototyping Global Brands

Overview


During my time at Omnicom Media Group, I designed interactive prototypes and digital experiences for some of the world’s most recognised consumer brands, delivered across the Omnicom network including the BBDO agency group.

The work spanned automotive, fast food, spirits, FMCG, and entertainment — six brand contexts, each with a different audience, different constraints, and a different definition of a successful digital experience.


BMW · McDonald’s · Sony · Bacardi · Pedigree · Ariel · Google

The Context

Brand guidelines enforced to the pixel Compressed feedback cycles Near-production prototype quality Multi-stakeholder alignment

Consumer brand work at this level operates under conditions that are different from product UX. The ability to prototype quickly, defend decisions with research, and iterate without losing craft quality was what made the work successful across six different brand contexts in the same role.

The Brands

BMW McDonald's Sony Motion Pictures Bacardi Pedigree Ariel Google (YouTube)

BMW

Interactive prototypes for digital campaign experiences. Automotive digital UX requires configuration tools, performance data, and immersive visuals to work together without the interface getting in the way.


McDonald's

UX and interaction design for consumer digital touchpoints. When the user journey appears simple, every friction point is immediately visible. The design has to be frictionless by default.


Sony Motion Pictures

Digital experience design for film campaign activations. Balancing immersive brand expression with clear calls to action — without obscuring the conversion path.


Bacardi

Age-gated experience design, global audience requirements, and brand expression within a regulated product category. All interactions prototyped and tested across device types before production.


Pedigree & Ariel

FMCG digital experiences designed to perform across markets and devices, with accessibility and cross-device consistency as central requirements. Rapid iteration across creative variants.


Google (YouTube)

Interaction design and prototyping for YouTube campaign experiences, held to Google’s standard for interaction quality and polish.

BBDO Network Redesign

In addition to the brand project work, I led the redesign of the BBDO network platform — applying design thinking methodology and facilitating cross-functional collaboration across a complex, global, multi-stakeholder project using Figma, Miro, and Mural across distributed teams in multiple time zones.

The redesign was recognised as a leading innovation initiative across Omnicom's global platforms. This project required presenting design decisions to groups of 60 or more designers and brand stakeholders across agencies.


Recognised as a leading innovation initiative across Omnicom’s global platforms.

Building UX Capability at Omnicom


40% reduction in external design dependency.

Before this project work could happen, UX as a discipline had to be established at Omnicom from the ground up. Starting from zero and building to a functioning, respected practice within a creative agency environment required making the case for research-led design to stakeholders accustomed to a different process.


Built from zero

Research methodology, design thinking tools, team practices, and documentation standards established from scratch.

40% less external dependency

In-house UX capability reduced reliance on external design agencies across all Omnicom accounts.

Faster delivery

Improved project delivery speed across all accounts by embedding UX directly into the agency workflow.
